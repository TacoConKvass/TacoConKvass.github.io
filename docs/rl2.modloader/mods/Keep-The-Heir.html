Keep The Heir

... allows you to continue the run you had just finished. The player doesn't have to watch the parade after beating the final boss to progress to the next New Game+ cycle. The character keeps all gathered relics with them when travelling to the next cycle. Gathered gold can be configured to be kept between NG+ cycles as well.
