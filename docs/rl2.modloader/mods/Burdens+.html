Burdens+

... allows the player to overcome the vanilla Burden limit, by giving evey Burden a maximum level of 99. Additionally a burden present only in True Rogue mode becomes available during normal gameplay:
- Burden Of Estuaries - Bosses gain commander buffs.

Some Burdens are not affected by the bonus levels.

When the level of the Burden of Scale is set too hihg, the game might fail to create a world on some seeds. Excersise caution.
